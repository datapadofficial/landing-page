## Prerequisites

<FeatureItem>**LinkedIn Account** - An active LinkedIn personal profile or company page with content publishing history</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Professional Content** - At least 30 days of posts, articles, or content engagement for analysis</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Connection History** - Network connections and follower data for meaningful professional insights</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Account Access** - Admin access to company pages (if analyzing company page performance)</FeatureItem>

## Connection Methods

### LinkedIn OAuth
Secure connection using LinkedIn's OAuth system with read-only access to your profile and content data.

### Company Page Access
Connect company pages for business-focused content analysis and team collaboration.

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization failed
If LinkedIn authorization fails:
- Ensure your LinkedIn account is in good standing with the platform
- Check that you have the necessary permissions for company page access
- Verify your account isn't restricted by LinkedIn's professional policies
- Try clearing browser cookies and reconnecting

### Missing content data
If content performance data is missing:
- Verify you have published content in the selected timeframe
- Check that posts have been live for at least 24-48 hours for complete analytics
- Ensure your profile has sufficient activity for meaningful insights
- Confirm content complies with LinkedIn's professional community guidelines

### Limited network insights
If network analysis appears limited:
- Verify you have sufficient connections for statistical analysis
- Check that your profile is set to show activity to your network
- Ensure connection privacy settings allow for insight generation
- Contact support if network data appears incomplete despite proper settings
