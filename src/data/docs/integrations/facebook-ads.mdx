## Prerequisites

<FeatureItem>**Facebook Business Account** - An active Facebook Business Manager account with ad spending history</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Ad Account Access** - Admin or Advertiser access to your Facebook Ad accounts</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Campaign Data** - At least 30 days of campaign data for meaningful optimization insights</FeatureItem>

## Connection Methods

### Facebook OAuth
Secure connection using Facebook's OAuth system with read-only permissions to your ad data.

### Business Manager Integration
Connect through Facebook Business Manager for multi-account access and team collaboration.

## Connection Guide

### Step 1: Access Facebook Ads Integration

Navigate to Integrations in Datapad and select Facebook Ads:

![Facebook Ads connect screen on Datapad UI](/images/docs/fbads-connect.png)

### Step 2: Authenticate with Facebook

Click "Connect with Facebook" and sign in with your Facebook Business account:

![Facebook Ads OAuth authentication](/images/docs/fbads-oauth.png)

### Step 3: Select Ad Accounts

Choose which Facebook Ad accounts you want to analyze:

![Facebook Ads account selection](/images/docs/fbads-account-select.png)

**Required Selections:**
- Facebook Business Account
- Ad Accounts to Connect
- Data Access Permissions

**Available Analysis:**
- Campaign Performance & ROAS
- Audience Demographics & Behavior
- Ad Creative Performance
- Conversion Tracking & Attribution
- Budget Utilization & Recommendations

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization failed
If Facebook authorization fails:
- Ensure you have admin or advertiser access to the ad accounts
- Check that your Facebook Business Manager account is properly set up
- Verify your account isn't restricted by Facebook policies
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Facebook account

### Missing campaign data
If campaign data is missing:
- Verify you selected the correct ad accounts during setup
- Check that campaigns have been running for at least 7 days
- Ensure you have sufficient spending history for analysis
- Verify Facebook Pixel is properly implemented for conversion tracking

### ROAS data unavailable
If ROAS data is missing:
- Ensure Facebook Pixel or Conversions API is properly set up
- Check that conversion events are being tracked correctly
- Verify purchase values are being passed to Facebook
- Contact support if custom conversion tracking needs configuration
