## Prerequisites

<FeatureItem>**TikTok Ads Manager** - An active TikTok Ads Manager account with campaign spending history</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Creative Content** - Video ad creatives and campaign data for performance pattern analysis</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Conversion Tracking** - TikTok Pixel properly implemented for ROI and conversion analysis</FeatureItem>

## Connection Methods

### TikTok Marketing API
Secure connection using TikTok's Marketing API with read-only access to campaign performance data.

### Business Center Integration
Connect through TikTok Business Center for multi-account access and team collaboration.

## Connection Guide

### Step 1: Access TikTok Ads Integration

Navigate to Integrations in Datapad and select TikTok Ads:

![TikTok Ads connect screen on Datapad UI](/images/docs/tads-connect.png)

### Step 2: Authenticate with TikTok

Click "Connect with TikTok" and sign in with your TikTok business account:

![TikTok Ads OAuth authentication](/images/docs/tads-oauth.png)

### Step 3: Select Ad Accounts

Choose which TikTok Ads accounts you want to analyze:

![TikTok Ads account selection](/images/docs/tads-account-select.png)

**Required Selections:**
- TikTok Business Account
- Ad Accounts to Connect
- Campaign Data Access Permissions

<StepItem>Datapad only requests read-only access to your TikTok Ads data and never modifies your campaigns, creatives, or targeting settings.</StepItem>

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization failed
If TikTok Ads authorization fails:
- Ensure you have admin or advertiser access to the TikTok ad accounts
- Check that your TikTok Business account is properly set up
- Verify your account complies with TikTok's advertising policies
- Try connecting through TikTok Business Center directly

### Missing campaign data
If campaign data is missing:
- Verify you selected the correct ad accounts during setup
- Check that campaigns have been running for at least 48-72 hours
- Ensure campaigns have sufficient spend for meaningful analytics
- Confirm TikTok Pixel is properly implemented for conversion tracking

### Creative analytics incomplete
If creative performance data is incomplete:
- Verify video ads have been approved and are actively serving
- Check that creative assets meet TikTok's advertising guidelines
- Ensure sufficient creative diversity for meaningful pattern analysis
- Contact support if specific creative metrics appear to be missing
