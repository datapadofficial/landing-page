## Prerequisites

<FeatureItem>**Instagram Business Account** - An active Instagram Business or Creator account with posting history</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Facebook Page Connection** - Instagram account connected to a Facebook Page for advanced insights</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Content History** - At least 30 days of posts and stories for meaningful pattern analysis</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Admin Access** - Admin access to both Instagram account and connected Facebook Page</FeatureItem>

## Connection Methods

### Instagram Business API
Connect through Facebook's Instagram Basic Display API for comprehensive business insights.

### Meta Business Integration
Connect through Meta Business Manager for multi-account access and team collaboration.

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization failed
If Instagram authorization fails:
- Ensure your Instagram account is set to Business or Creator mode
- Verify your Instagram account is connected to a Facebook Page
- Check that you have admin access to both accounts
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting through Meta Business Manager

### Missing insights data
If insights data is missing:
- Verify your Instagram account has been in Business mode for at least 24 hours
- Check that your account meets Instagram's minimum requirements for insights
- Ensure posts have been published for sufficient time to generate data
- Confirm your account isn't restricted by Instagram policies

### Limited data access
If you're seeing limited data:
- Verify the Facebook Page connection is properly configured
- Check that Instagram Professional Dashboard shows insights data
- Ensure you have the necessary permissions on the connected Facebook Page
- Contact support if data appears incomplete after proper setup
