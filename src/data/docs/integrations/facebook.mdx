## Prerequisites

<FeatureItem>**Facebook Page** - An active Facebook Business Page with posting history and audience engagement</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Admin Access** - Admin or Editor access to your Facebook Page for data authorization</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Facebook Account** - A personal Facebook account connected to your business page</FeatureItem>
<FeatureItem>**Content History** - At least 30 days of posts and engagement data for meaningful analysis</FeatureItem>

## Connection Methods

### Facebook OAuth
Secure connection using Facebook's OAuth system with read-only access to your page data.

### Facebook Business Manager
Connect through Business Manager for multi-page access and team collaboration.

## Behind the Scenes

Datapad connects to the Facebook Graph API to fetch your page insights, post performance data, and audience demographics. Our AI analyzes engagement patterns, content performance, and audience behavior to provide actionable recommendations for improving your Facebook page's reach and engagement.

## Troubleshooting

### Authorization failed
If Facebook authorization fails:
- Ensure you have admin or editor access to the Facebook Pages
- Check that your Facebook account isn't restricted by platform policies
- Verify your pages comply with Facebook's community standards
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Facebook account

### Missing page data
If page data is missing:
- Verify you selected the correct pages during setup
- Check that pages have sufficient posting history for analysis
- Ensure pages have audience engagement and insights data
- Confirm pages are published and publicly accessible

### Engagement data incomplete
If engagement data appears incomplete:
- Verify Facebook Page Insights are enabled for your pages
- Check that posts have been published for at least 24-48 hours
- Ensure pages meet Facebook's minimum requirements for insights
- Contact support if specific metrics appear to be missing
